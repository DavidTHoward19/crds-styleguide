Card Decks

A card deck is a group of cards which share the same height as the tallest card in the deck. Height is determined by the length of content within a card, and the card with the most content will dictate the height of the other cards in the deck.

Default Card Deck

On mobile-sized screens, a default card deck is one row of many cards in a scrollable container. On table-sized screens and larger, every fourth card will cause the layout to wrap to a new line.

Card Deck Carousel

The .card-deck--carousel class is a modifier class for card decks. Instead of wrapping overflow cards to a new line on tablet-sized screens (as the default .card-deck does), a card deck carousel will keep all cards in one (scrollable) row regardless of screen size.
