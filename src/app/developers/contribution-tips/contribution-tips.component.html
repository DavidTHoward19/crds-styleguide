Contribution Tips

Contributions to crds-styles and/or crds-styleguide (the DDK) are encouraged and may be facilitated through Github's pull-request feature.

If you don't have publishing rights to the repository you'd like to contribute to, simply fork the repository to your own account and submit a pull-request that describes the nature of your changes and what features and/or defects it addresses.

Or you can just send an email to design@crossroads.net.
